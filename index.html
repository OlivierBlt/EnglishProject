Why it matters

An overlooked waste stream can become a visible, tactile design object.

Circular logic

From waste to object

Human hairs are continuously produced and continuously discarded. We reframe that flow as a material resource.

Material angle

Design value

Care Guide

Simple maintenance for a material with an unusual story.

Cleaning

Shampoo method

  • Use lukewarm water with a small amount of mild shampoo.
  • Apply lightly with a soft sponge.
  • Wipe with a damp cloth and air-dry flat.
Weekly care

Gentle brushing & vacuuming

  • Vacuum gently on low or medium suction.
  • Avoid aggressive rotating brushes.
  • Handle the surface with soft, regular care.

FAQ

The project is surprising, so the questions are part of the experience.

Why use human hairs as a material?

Because they are already produced in large quantities and usually discarded immediately. The project rethinks that material flow.

Is this a design project or a research project?

Both. It sits between circular material research, product experimentation, and storytelling through design.

How do you clean the rugs?

With gentle vacuuming and a mild shampoo cleaning method using lukewarm water and soft handling.
